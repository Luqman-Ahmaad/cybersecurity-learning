# 🧑‍💻 Types of Hackers

## 🎯 Objective
To understand different types of hackers and their intentions in cyberspace.

---

## 🧠 What is a Hacker?

A hacker is an individual who uses technical skills to gain access to systems, networks, or data.

Hackers can be ethical or malicious depending on their intent.

---

## 🟢 1. White Hat Hacker (Ethical Hacker)

- Authorized professionals
- Work legally to secure systems
- Perform penetration testing and vulnerability assessments

### ✅ Examples:
- Security analysts
- Penetration testers

---

## ⚫ 2. Black Hat Hacker

- Malicious attackers
- Break into systems illegally
- Steal, damage, or exploit data

### ❌ Activities:
- Data theft
- Ransomware attacks
- Financial fraud

---

## ⚪ 3. Grey Hat Hacker

- Operate between white and black hat
- May hack without permission but do not intend harm
- Often report vulnerabilities after discovering them

---

## 🎭 4. Script Kiddies

- Beginners with little knowledge
- Use pre-made tools/scripts
- Do not fully understand attacks

---

## 🏴‍☠️ 5. Hacktivists

- Hack for political or social causes
- Target governments or organizations

---

## 💼 6. State-Sponsored Hackers

- Work for governments
- Conduct cyber espionage or cyber warfare

---

## 🏢 7. Insider Threats

- Employees or trusted individuals
- Abuse access for malicious purposes

---

## 🧠 What I Learned

- Not all hackers are criminals
- Intent defines the type of hacker
- Ethical hacking is a legal and valuable career

---

## ⚠️ Ethical Note

- Always perform hacking activities legally
- Only test systems you own or have permission to test

---

## ✅ Conclusion

Understanding hacker types helps in identifying threats and building defensive strategies in cybersecurity.
