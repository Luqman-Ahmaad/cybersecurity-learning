# 🌐 Cyberspace Basics

## 🎯 Objective
To understand the concept of cyberspace and its importance in cybersecurity.

---

## 🌍 What is Cyberspace?

Cyberspace refers to the virtual environment created by interconnected computer systems, networks, and digital communication technologies.

It includes:
- The Internet
- Private networks
- Cloud systems
- IoT devices
- Digital communication platforms

---

## 🧠 Key Components of Cyberspace

### 1. Hardware
- Computers
- Servers
- Routers
- Mobile devices

### 2. Software
- Operating systems
- Applications
- Security tools

### 3. Networks
- LAN (Local Area Network)
- WAN (Wide Area Network)
- Internet

### 4. Data
- Personal information
- Business data
- Financial records

---

## 🔐 Importance of Cybersecurity in Cyberspace

Cyberspace is vulnerable to:
- Data breaches
- Malware attacks
- Unauthorized access
- Identity theft

Cybersecurity ensures:
- Confidentiality
- Integrity
- Availability (CIA Triad)

---

## ⚠️ Threats in Cyberspace

- Hackers
- Malware
- Phishing attacks
- Insider threats

---

## 🧠 What I Learned

- Cyberspace is not just the internet — it's a complete digital ecosystem
- Security is essential at every level (hardware, software, network)
- Understanding cyberspace is the foundation of cybersecurity

---

## ✅ Conclusion

Cyberspace is the backbone of modern digital communication. Securing it is critical to protect data, systems, and users from cyber threats.
