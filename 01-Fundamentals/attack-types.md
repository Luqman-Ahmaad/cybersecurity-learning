# ⚔️ Types of Cyber Attacks

## 🎯 Objective
To understand different types of cyber attacks and how attackers exploit systems.

---

## 🧠 What is a Cyber Attack?

A cyber attack is an attempt to gain unauthorized access, disrupt, or damage a system, network, or data.

---

## 🔥 Common Types of Cyber Attacks

### 1. Phishing Attack
- Tricks users into revealing sensitive information
- Usually done via fake emails or websites

### 🔍 Example:
- Fake login pages

---

### 2. Malware Attack
- Uses malicious software to damage or control systems

### 🔍 Includes:
- Viruses
- Worms
- Trojans

---

### 3. Denial of Service (DoS)
- Overloads a system to make it unavailable

---

### 4. Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS)
- Multiple systems attack one target simultaneously

---

### 5. Man-in-the-Middle (MITM)
- Attacker intercepts communication between two parties

---

### 6. SQL Injection
- Injects malicious SQL queries into input fields
- Can access or modify database data

---

### 7. Cross-Site Scripting (XSS)
- Injects malicious scripts into web pages
- Affects users visiting the page

---

### 8. Password Attacks
- Attempts to guess or crack passwords

### 🔍 Types:
- Brute force
- Dictionary attack

---

### 9. Zero-Day Attack
- Exploits unknown vulnerabilities
- No patch available

---

### 10. Insider Attack
- Performed by someone inside the organization

---

## ⚠️ Attack Vectors

- Email
- Websites
- Network connections
- Removable devices

---

## 🧠 What I Learned

- Attacks target different layers (network, application, human)
- Many attacks rely on human mistakes
- Understanding attacks helps in building defenses

---

## 🔐 Basic Prevention

- Strong passwords
- Software updates
- User awareness
- Firewalls and IDS/IPS

---

## ✅ Conclusion

Cyber attacks are diverse and constantly evolving. Understanding them is essential for effective cybersecurity defense.
